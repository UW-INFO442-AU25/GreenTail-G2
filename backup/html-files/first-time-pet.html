What does "organic" mean for pet food?

  • Fewer synthetic pesticides on crops/ingredients.
  • Third-party certification improves transparency.
  • Doesn't replace vet advice—still pick what fits your pet.

First-week routine

  • Day 1–2: quiet space, water, gentle play
  • Day 3–4: vet appointment scheduled
  • Day 5–7: begin food transition (25➝50➝75➝100%)

Quick FAQ

How often should I feed?
2–3 meals/day; follow label by weight.

When to call the vet?
Vomiting/diarrhea >24h, no appetite, lethargy.
